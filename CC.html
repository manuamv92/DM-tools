Character Creator

SRD5 stands for the "System Reference Document 5", which is a set of rules and guidelines for creating and playing a character in the fifth edition of this popular tabletop role-playing game. Here are the basic steps for creating an SRD5 character:
1. Choose a race.
In SRD5, you can play as a variety of different races, each with their own unique abilities and characteristics. Some examples of races include humans, elves, dwarves, and halflings.
2. Choose a class.
Your character's class determines their role in the party and what abilities they have access to. Some examples of classes include fighters, wizards, and rogues.
3. Determine ability scores.
Each character has six ability scores that represent their physical and mental abilities. These scores are strength, dexterity, constitution, intelligence, wisdom, and charisma. You can roll dice to determine your scores randomly, or use a point buy system to assign them yourself, your race and class heavily influe in your ability scores, please, check the rules section to determine wich class and race fits your ability scores.
4. Select skills and feats.
Skills represent your character's training and expertise in various areas, such as perception or stealth. Feats are special abilities or enhancements that your character can choose to gain.
5. Finalize details.
Your character starts with a certain amount of gold to spend on equipment and gear. You can choose from a variety of weapons, armor, and other items to outfit your character, also, give your character a name, appearance, and background story to round out their character.
Congratulations, you have created your first SRD5 character!.
